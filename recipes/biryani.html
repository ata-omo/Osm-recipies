About biryani!!

this is chicken biryani made from chicken obviously!!!! it's a non- veg dish so we request all the vegetarians to try our salad instead or you can also try our sandwich

Ingredients

  • basmati rice
  • chicken
  • rose water
  • kevra water
  • cinnamon
  • cardamom(small/black)
  • salt!!!
  • onion (very very important)
  • biryani masala

Steps to prepare

    wash every thing cook rice 80% cook chiken 80% seperately mix them and cook for 30 min on low flame finish it completely irrespective of its taste.
